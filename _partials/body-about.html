ABOUT US

People who treat your home loan like their own.

Supreme Lending has helped families across the country move into homes that fit their lives — from first apartments and starter houses to farmhouses and forever homes.

OUR STORY

Founded on relationships.

Founded in 1999 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas, Supreme Lending — a d/b/a of Everett Financial, Inc. — has grown into one of the country's leading independent residential mortgage lenders. We are licensed in all 50 states and Puerto Rico and serve borrowers through hundreds of branches and licensed loan officers.

Our growth has always been driven by people: loan officers who know their neighborhoods, processors who treat every file with care, and operations teams that move quickly so closings stay on track.
